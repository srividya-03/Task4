About us

At Cafe Elegante, everything is made from scratch using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Our menu is extensive and includes a wide range of bread, pastries, cakes, cookies, and other baked treats, fast food like pizzas, burgers, hot dogs, and pastas and main courses. Whether you're looking for a classic sourdough loaf, a flaky croissant, or a decadent chocolate cake,Cafe Elegante has got you covered.

What sets Cafe Elegante apart is its commitment to quality and tradition. We use traditional baking methods and recipes that have earned us the love of foodies, ensuring that every product is made with the same care and attention to detail as it was when the bakery first opened its doors.

The atmosphere at Glen's Bakehouse is warm and welcoming, with friendly staff who are always happy to help customers find the perfect treat. The bakery is known for its cozy seating area, where customers can enjoy a cup of coffee and a pastry while watching the bakers at work in the open kitchen.
